Butt welding fittings
manufacturer

Since 1971.

Today IRC is a one of the most important
companies for the production of butt welding
fittings.

In 50 years, the company has become an important reality in the Italian market and has taken a leading role
at a global level, constantly increasing exports throughout Europe and in strategic markets in the Middle
East, Asia, Australia and South America.

Products

Butt welding fittings in carbon, alloy
and stainless steel

Range from ½” to 48″ and higher

In welded and seamless construction, in different thicknesses and grades of steel material conforming to ASME and EN standards; certified in accordance with the requirements of the EN 10204:2004 standard and the PED Directive 2014/68/EU.

Guaranteed and certified quality.

IRC has made quality its greatest competitive advantage, because quality distinguishes, and is the basis
of a good reputation.

A principle that is applied to every phase of production: from business processes to the product, from customer care to supply systems: each phase is monitored under the magnifying glass of a very high standard, the only one possible.

IRC fittings are guaranteed by numerous tests and checks carried out on the final product.

Our Projects
